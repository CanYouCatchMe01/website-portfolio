AI generated bike frame

Marcus Cazzola
February 5, 2023
Updated on March 22, 2023

Details:

  • Made in the CAD program Fusion 360
  • Generative design AI model

I'm using Fusion 360 Generative Design model to create the most optimal bike frame which is both very light and durable.

The AI model works by allowing the user to create shapes that they would like to connect and specify the amount of force each shape will endure. The AI then connects the shapes and intelligently removes material from areas of low stress.
