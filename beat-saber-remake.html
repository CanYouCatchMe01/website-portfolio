Details:

  • Inspired by Beat Saber.
  • Custom level creation tool to create new levels fast.

Fast level creation tool:

In the normal Beat Saber, every cube is placed out manually by hand to make a level. This makes the level creation, of one song, take many hours.

I created a tool that lets the developer “records” a song. The developer records the song by swinging his sabers and pressing the trigger button. Then the position, time, color, and sword travel direction gets stored in a file. Then the file can get played back and someone can play the level.
